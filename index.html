About Hallulies Pub

Hallulies Pub is your premier destination in Asufufu-Sunyani for luxury accommodation, exquisite dining, and memorable events. We offer the perfect venue for birthdays, weddings, baby showers, parties, and more!

Our facility features luxury rooms, car park, recreational hall, bar/lounge, and Airbnb options. We specialize in local delicacies, pizza, continental dishes, beverages, and grilled items.
