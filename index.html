About Hallulies Experience

Hallulies is your premier destination in Asufufu-Sunyani for luxury accommodation, exquisite dining, and memorable events. We offer the perfect venue for birthdays, weddings, baby showers, parties, and more!

Our facility features luxury rooms, secure parking, recreational spaces, bar/lounge, and flexible event options. We specialize in local delicacies, continental dishes, beverages, and premium grilled items.
